About the Firm

A Practice Built on
Principle & Purpose

Pulkit Sanghi & Associates is a Delhi-based law firm offering comprehensive legal services across a wide spectrum of civil, criminal, and special matters. Founded in 2022, the firm has rapidly established a reputation for diligent advocacy and client-centric counsel.

Operating before the Courts of Delhi — including the District Courts, Consumer Forums, Labour Courts, and various Tribunals — we bring focused legal expertise and a thorough understanding of the evolving Indian legal landscape to every matter entrusted to us.

We believe that every client deserves sincere attention, transparent communication, and relentless representation. Whether the matter is routine or complex, we approach it with the same dedication to excellence.

What We Do

Practice Areas

01 ⚖️
Civil Litigation

Property disputes, injunctions, declarations, suits for recovery, partition matters, and all civil proceedings before courts of competent jurisdiction.

02 🏛️
Criminal Defence

Bail applications, anticipatory bail, trial defence, appeals, and revisions in criminal matters under IPC, BNSS, and special statutes.

03 💍
Matrimonial Matters

Divorce proceedings, judicial separation, maintenance & alimony, child custody, domestic violence cases, and matrimonial property disputes.

04 📃
Cheque Bounce (NI Act)

Comprehensive representation in Section 138 NI Act cases — from filing of complaints and trial to appeals — for both complainants and accused persons.

05 👷
Labour & Employment

Disputes before Labour Courts and Industrial Tribunals involving wrongful termination, workmen compensation, retrenchment, and service matters.

06 🛡️
Consumer Protection

Representing consumers before District, State, and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commissions in matters of deficiency of service and unfair trade practices.
